Wrassling

Start rotating your arms and try to throw your opponents out of the ring in Wrassling. Play a challenging 1 player mode in which you can unlock new achievements like hats and play a local 2 player match against a friend in VS or Co-op Mode. The wrassle boss mode is exclusive to 2player.com! Controls: 1P mode: Use Z and X to rotate arms and arrow keys to move and jump. 2P mode: Player 1 use WASD and X, C and Player 2: Use arrow keys and O, P.
