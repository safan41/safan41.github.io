Unfair Mario

8.0 - 906 ratings
mario  -  platform

Never has a game been so frustrating, yet so addictive, as this one. Unfair Mario takes the adventures of everyone's favorite plumber and flips them into a world of surprise falls, sudden traps and slew of other unexpected instant deaths! You'll be torn between wanting to smash your keyboard or hit that end goal just inches away. There are infinite lives, because you'll need every one. In Unfair Mario nothing is as it seems anymore... Find the final flag while avoiding the hidden traps. Have fun(?) with the unfair version of the classic mario platformer.

Features

  • Classic Super Mario World style graphics
  • Enormously challenging gameplay, unlike any Mario game to date
  • Simple controls
